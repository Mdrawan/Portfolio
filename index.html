Teknaf

As this is my hometown, I've visited this place so many time. Teknaf is an Upazila of Cox's Bazar District in the Division of Chittagong, Bangladesh. It forms the southernmost point in mainland Bangladesh. The name of the region comes from the Naf River which forms the Eastern boundary of the upazila. It shares the border with Burma.

The biggest attraction of Teknaf, Cox's Bazar is a ride by a boat or a sampan in the Naaf River.Those of you who want to know about the culture of Arakans and the Mogh communities they should never miss an opportunity to visit Teknaf, Cox's Bazar. The natural beauty of Teknaf, Cox's Bazar is outstanding. The wide sandy beaches and the green hills acting as backdrop provide scenery which cannot be forgotten easily. Watching migratory birds is a preoccupation of many tourists visiting this place.

Kuakata

Kuakata is one the most beautiful sea beach of Bangladesh. The district Potuakhali is called the "Daughter of Sea" beacause of Kuakata. It is in southeastern Bangladesh and is the number sizeable tourist destination in the country.

The top attractions to visit in Kuakata, locally known as "Shagor Kannya" - Kuakata Sea Beach, Lebu Chor, Jhau Forest, Gangamati Reserved Forest.
